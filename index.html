Education

Teach Success with Web3 - Introducing Education DAOs

  • Store and validate learning
    credentials on the blockchain.
  • Reward competence-based knowledge
    as opposed to time spent in a
    classroom with micro-transactions to
    validate learning.
  • Reimagine the future of education
    where access is not a limitation.
  • Substantially reduced cost of
    education for students
  • Education is not constrained by time
    or location. Students and teachers
    only require access to the internet to
    learn or to be taught.
  • Students can join courses that align
    with their interests and navigate their
    learning journey. After completing a
    course, the student can give back
    within the course or peer-review other
    students’ work.
  • Decentralized educational
    experiences.

  • Experiential Learning

    Create an immersive, interactive digital world to enhance the delivery of lessons and educate the invisible.

  • Reward Students

    Airdrop students NFTs and tokens for participating or completing work to increase motivation and interest in learning.

  • Game-based learning

    Games can engage learners by adapting difficulty based on need and proficiency.

  • Revolutionize Learning

    Teach the dangerous, impossible, counterproductive and expensive in the metaverse.

A New Way to Educate

Teacher
  • Teachers can build immersive learning environments to deliver lessons in a 3D digital space that trains motor skills and enhances muscular memory to encourage learnings to become second nature.
  • Practice stimulated real-world and high-pressure scenarios in a safe environment.
  • Educate with storytelling and vivid visualization.
  • Lesson plans and courses can be minted as NFTs and sold or traded in the marketplace. The IP belongs to the author.
  • Network with teachers globally.
Student
  • Visit a virtual campus or classroom to meet tutors, visit libraries or hang out with classmates independent of your physical location.
  • Gamified learning environments increase the efficiency of learning.
  • Network with a diverse and global community of students.
  • Store and validate educational credentials on the blockchain. Build yourself a digital education passport.
  • Learn to Earn
    • Be incentivized to complete assignments.
    • Be incentivized to peer review work.
    • Be incentivized to attend virtual classes with in-class pop quizzes.
